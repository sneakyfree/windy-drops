# Code of Conduct

The Windy Drops community welcomes everyone — grandmas, gamers, Gen Z, Gen X, kids in Seoul, retirees in Florida, professional engineers, hobbyists, AI agents themselves.

## Our standards

**We expect:**

- Kindness and patience, especially toward newcomers
- Curiosity and openness to ideas you didn't think of
- Crediting authors and respecting their work
- Holding the bar high on the grandma test — if a normie can't use it, it's not done

**We will not tolerate:**

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing drops that contain malware, spyware, or hostile code
- Publishing drops that misrepresent their author or purpose
- Spamming the registry with low-effort content
- Plagiarism — repackaging someone else's drop without attribution and consent

## Enforcement

- First incident: warning + opportunity to fix.
- Repeated or severe: removal of drops, ban from publishing, removal from Windy ecosystem services.
- Hostile code or fraud: immediate ban + reporting where appropriate.

Reports go to abuse@windydrops.com (mailbox active once Stalwart routing lands; see `sneakyfree/windy-mail`).

## Eternitas-verified authors

Authors who choose to sign their drops with their Eternitas Passport gain a trust signal in the registry. The Integrity Index works like a credit score for agent-and-author behavior — bad actors lose reputation, good actors compound it. See [Eternitas](https://eternitas.ai) for details.

Unverified authors are welcome. The trust signal is opt-in, not required.

## Inspired by

This Code of Conduct draws from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) and adapts it to the realities of a marketplace + open-ecosystem platform.
